About Me

Who I Am

Dedicated engineering student bridging theory with real-world automation solutions.

I am Avijit Chakroborty, a dedicated Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at Sylhet Engineering College with a passion for robotics and automation. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree with a CGPA of 3.77/4.0, I am working on my thesis titled "A Deep Learning Approach to Build a Lightweight Skin Diseases Classifier by Means of Transfer Learning."

My journey in engineering has been marked by hands-on experience in embedded systems, power electronics, and machine learning. I have actively participated in various robotics competitions and served as an instructor at our college's Robotics Club, sharing knowledge and fostering innovation among peers.

With expertise in tools like Proteus, ESP32, Arduino, MATLAB, and Python, I enjoy creating practical solutions that bridge theoretical knowledge with real-world applications. My goal is to contribute to the advancement of automation and smart systems technology.
